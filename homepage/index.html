Introduction

Hi! Welcome to my homepage, which I've made for Problem Set 8 of CS50x, a fantastic Computer Science course by Harvard. I first tried the course way back in 2019, when I was still 12 and didn't have a good grasp of the crucial concepts of programming, so I found the problem sets bafflingly difficult. I stopped after Week 2. Two years passed and during the summer holidays this year, I decided to give it another try. I'm doing much better this time around, and I aim to finish the Final Project by December.

I plan to use this as my personal page, and will continually update it with new posts, projects and features. In the meantime, feel free to explore around.
